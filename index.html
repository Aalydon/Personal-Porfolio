About me

I am a Software Game Developer with hands-on experience making simulators for Navy personnel on contracts with the Canadian Department of Defence. These simulators and Applications ranged from motion-platform gyroscopic simulators in VR, to desktop click-and-play task-trainers for teaching Navy students their curriculum.

I graduated with a B.Sc. in Computer Science (Software Engineering) at the University of Victoria in 2019, and have been working Full-Time since Feb of 2020.

My hobbies center around gaming and other media such as live-streaming. My pursuit is to work Full-Time in a game studio where I am surrounded by game developers that share my passion for games, and to collaborate on making a game that we would feel proud playing together.
